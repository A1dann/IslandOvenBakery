Our Locations

ORIGINAL

Port of Spain Branch

Our original Port of Spain branch offers fresh breads and pastries daily in a warm, welcoming setting. A convenient spot for a quick stop or relaxed visit. Open Monday to Saturday, 7:30 AM – 5:30 PM. Located at 42 Cinnamon Boulevard, Woodbrook.

NEW

San Fernando Branch

Serving the southern community, our San Fernando branch offers the same freshly baked breads and pastries in a relaxed and friendly environment. Open Monday to Saturday, 7:00 AM – 6:00 PM. Located at 15 Golden Crust Drive, Gulf View.

NEW

Arima Branch

Located in the vibrant town of Arima, this branch brings our fresh breads and pastries to the east, offering a convenient and welcoming stop for customers on the go. Open Monday to Saturday, 7:00 AM – 6:00 PM. Located at 88 Pimento Street, Santa Rosa.

NEW

Chaguanas Branch

Situated in the bustling central area, our Chaguanas branch provides quick access to fresh breads and pastries, perfect for busy customers seeking quality and convenience. Open Monday to Saturday, 7:00 AM – 6:00 PM. Located at 102 Nutmeg Avenue, Endeavour.
